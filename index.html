MODIS Earth Explorer

Explore real NASA MODIS satellite imagery through NDVI, wildfire detections, and land surface temperature layers from NASA GIBS.

MODIS Terra NDVI 8-Day

How does vegetation change across seasons?

NDVI highlights vegetation density. Move through the year to see seasonal greenness patterns across the globe or zoom into a specific region.

MODIS Active Fire Layer

Where are active fire detections visible?

This view overlays MODIS active fire detections on true-color satellite imagery. Change the date and region to compare fire activity spatially.

MODIS Terra Land Surface Temperature

How does surface heat vary by place and date?

Land surface temperature shows how hot the Earth’s surface is, which can differ from air temperature. This is useful for comparing deserts, cities, forests, and polar regions.

Data note

The maps are loaded directly from NASA Global Imagery Browse Services using MODIS WMS layers.

The time slider changes the WMS TIME parameter, so the image updates to the selected date.

The region selector changes the WMS BBOX parameter, creating a spatial filter without server-side code.
