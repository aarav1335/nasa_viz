DSC 106 Project 3 Checkpoint

MODIS Earth Explorer

Six early exploratory views using NASA MODIS imagery from NASA Global Imagery Browse Services (GIBS).

Graphs 1–2 · MODIS Terra NDVI 8-Day

How does seasonal vegetation change across regions?

NDVI is a satellite-derived vegetation index. This early prototype compares MODIS greenness imagery by month and region.

Graphs 3–4 · MODIS Active Fire Detections

Where do active fire detections appear over time?

This view overlays MODIS fire detections on true-color MODIS imagery. It is best viewed over fire-prone regions such as California or Australia.

Graphs 5–6 · MODIS Terra Land Surface Temperature

How does land surface heat vary across regions and seasons?

Land surface temperature measures the temperature of the Earth’s surface, not the air. This helps compare deserts, cities, forests, and high-latitude regions.

Checkpoint data/design notes

Dataset: NASA MODIS imagery served through NASA GIBS WMS.

Interaction: month/date filters, region filters, layer toggles, and details-on-hover.

Next step: narrow the final project to one strong question, likely seasonal vegetation change or fire/vegetation recovery.
