DSC 106 Project 3

MODIS visualization concept gallery

Prototype data shaped like MODIS products, ready to replace with real NASA layers.

Option 1

How green is the planet over the year?

A seasonal NDVI view. The map shows simulated monthly vegetation intensity by biome, while the line chart compares how different places green up and fade through the year.

Option 3

Wildfire scars before and after

A before, burn, and recovery sequence. This lets viewers scrub through a fire event, compare burn severity, and see vegetation rebound after the peak damage.

Option 8

Two decades of Earth from MODIS

A story selector for long-term landscape change. Pick a place, move through time, and compare the current snapshot against the full trend.

How to choose

Greenness is best if you want a clean, global, seasonal story.

Fire scars is best if you want a dramatic before/after narrative.

Earth change is best if you want a broader “MODIS over time” project with multiple case studies.
