Land surface temperature

Prototype question 3: How does surface heat vary across regions and seasons?

Land surface temperature is not air temperature; it measures the heat of the land surface. It is helpful for comparing deserts, cities, forests, and monsoon regions.

Checkpoint interpretation

Most promising final direction

The strongest final path is probably the NDVI/greenness story because it has a clear seasonal question, easy interaction, and visible regional differences. Fires and temperature are useful supporting layers, but they currently feel more like side explorations.

Data: NASA GIBS WMS layers loaded directly in the browser.

Interaction: region dropdown, month slider, date filters, hover tooltips, and small-multiple comparisons.

Next step: decide whether the final project focuses on NDVI seasonality or fire/vegetation recovery.
