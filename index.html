Checkpoint interpretation

Why this direction is stronger

Clearer visual story: the temperature layer produces more readable differences across regions and seasons than the earlier NDVI and fire layers.

Good interaction: the region dropdown changes the spatial frame, while the date picker changes the temporal frame.

Next step: for the final project, this could become a more polished comparison of hot, dry, tropical, and urban regions.
